It's all about HOW communication occurs

  • Demonstrate asynchronous: Write 'What is the weather like right now? Please write the answer and drop it on a desk near me NOW!' on a piece of paper, fold it and give it to a student.
  • Do some other stuff, until they drop off the answer
  • Demonstrate synchronous: Ask a student, ***"What is the time?"***, and wait for the response
  • Compare our cook telling the dish washer "Wash this pot" / thread performing synchronous and asynchronous communication
